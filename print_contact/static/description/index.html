print_contact

Printing my contacts

This module offers you various printed reports to present your contact

Select partners in the tree-view contact-list and select the PDF you want to print

This buttons are also available in the contact form for quick printing process.

PDFs

List of your contact

This pdf will show the contacts as a list, with a way to get in touch with them through their phone, mobile or email.

Check-in

This pdf allows you to get a check-in report file of your clients, with a checkbox to sign and confirm his presence.

Full page

A completed A4 page per person used to show-up his photo with his address and phone-contact.
